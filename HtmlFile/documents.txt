Block/Inline Element
Block Elements:-
            New Line:- Starts on a new line.
             Full Width:Take up all horizontal space.
             Styling: can have margins and padding.
             Size: width and height can be set.
             Example:
             <div><p><h1><ul><li>
Inline Element:-
             flow: stay in line with taxt.
             width: just as wide as the content .
             No break: no new line Between.
             Limited Styling: Can't space easily .
             Example:<span><em><img><Strong><a>
